Business Context

The Problem Banks Face Today

Financial fraud costs the global banking industry billions every year. Traditional rule-based systems generate high false-positive rates, slow investigations, and decisions that are difficult to defend in audits or legal proceedings.

Banks need more than a binary "approve or deny" engine. They need a platform that protects customers in real time, gives investigators a clear trail of evidence, and quantifies the financial impact of every decision — all while staying compliant with regulatory expectations.

Our Approach

A Complete Fraud & AML Security Layer

This platform sits between a bank's payment channels and its core systems. Every transfer and card transaction is scored in milliseconds by a hybrid AI engine — combining machine learning with explainable business rules — before any money moves.

Instead of a single "fraud score," the system returns a graded decision with reason codes, automatically opens forensic cases for suspicious activity, and gives operations teams a live dashboard to monitor risk posture across the entire institution.

Architecture

Four Integrated Components

Each layer serves a distinct business function — from real-time scoring to courtroom-ready evidence export.

🏦

Transfer API

The banking channel layer. Validates and routes every transfer request through fraud scoring before execution — blocking suspicious payments instantly.

🧠

AI Model Service

Hybrid ML + rules engine trained on real fraud datasets. Scores transfers and card transactions with velocity checks, device signals, and explainable reason codes.

🔐

Fraud API & Forensics

Core decision engine with case management, audit timelines, evidence boards, and signed ZIP export bundles (JSON + PDF + SHA-256 manifest) for legal proceedings.

📊

Operations Dashboard (Afra Gate)

Executive KPIs, geo risk hotspots, live alerts, investigator workflows, and model feedback loops — all in one glass-morphism command center.

ROI & Impact

Measurable Business Value

The platform is designed to deliver outcomes that bank executives and compliance officers can track and defend:

  • ✓ Loss prevention: Real-time blocking of high-risk transfers and card transactions, with estimated SAR loss prevented tracked on the dashboard.
  • ✓ Reduced investigation time: Auto-opened forensic cases with structured evidence boards, operator notes, and tiered investigation queues (Tier 1 → Escalation).
  • ✓ Regulatory readiness: Every decision is logged with model version, reason codes, and a tamper-evident export bundle suitable for audit and legal review.
  • ✓ Explainable AI: Decisions come with human-readable reason codes — not opaque scores — building trust with customers and regulators.
  • ✓ Continuous improvement: Investigators can submit model feedback and case dispositions (False Positive, True Positive, Law Enforcement) to refine the AI over time.
  • ✓ Geo-intelligence: Risk hotspot mapping across countries and cities helps prioritize AML and fraud resources where they matter most.
